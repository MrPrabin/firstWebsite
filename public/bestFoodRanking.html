Best Foods To Try In Bhutan

My favourite Top 3 Best Food To Try In Bhutan

  1. Ema Datsi (chillie with cheese)

    Eating rice without chillies is incomplete in Bhutan. Red or green chilles are just cooked with onions, garlic and cheese, it is super hot and very easy to make and as deslicious as it sounds like.

  2. Momo (dumpling)

    Stuffings mostly made of cabbage with cheese or minced beef with, onions, carrot and garlic and are stuffed in a wheat dough and it's steamed cooked or fried and eaten with ezay(bhutanese chillie paste).

  3. Bathup (wheat noodles with soup)

    Wheat dough cut into thin strips and cooked with the soup of your choice with some ganish and it taste best when eaten in a cold weather.
